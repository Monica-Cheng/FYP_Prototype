Instructions
1
Lie flat on a bench, grip barbell slightly wider than shoulder-width.
2
Unrack and lower the bar slowly to your mid-chest.
3
Press the bar back up in a slight arc until arms are fully extended.
4
Keep feet flat on the floor and back slightly arched throughout.
Safety Tips
Use a spotter for heavy sets. Don't bounce the bar off your chest. Control the descent for 2-3 seconds.
